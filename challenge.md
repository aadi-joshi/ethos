# Ethos AI
## Google Solution Challenge 2026 India Submission Context

---

## 1. Challenge Track

**Selected Track:** Unbiased AI Decision  
**Theme:** Ensuring Fairness and Detecting Bias in Automated Decisions

---

## 2. Context and Motivation

Artificial intelligence systems are increasingly used to make high-stakes decisions that directly impact individuals and communities. These include hiring processes, loan approvals, insurance assessments, and access to healthcare services.

However, these systems often inherit biases present in historical data. Without proper evaluation, machine learning models can unintentionally reinforce discrimination, leading to unfair and unethical outcomes.

Organizations face several challenges:

- Lack of visibility into model decision behavior  
- Difficulty identifying hidden bias in datasets  
- Limited tools for explaining fairness issues  
- Absence of actionable guidance to correct bias  

This creates a significant gap between AI adoption and responsible AI deployment.

---

## 3. Problem Definition

The core problem addressed by Ethos AI is:

> How can organizations effectively detect, understand, and mitigate bias in AI systems before they impact real-world decisions?

Current approaches are either too technical, fragmented, or limited to surface-level analysis. There is a need for a unified platform that simplifies fairness evaluation while providing meaningful insights and corrective strategies.

---

## 4. Proposed Solution

Ethos AI is an intelligent auditing platform that enables organizations to:

- Analyze datasets and model predictions for bias  
- Identify disparities across demographic groups  
- Understand the root causes of unfair outcomes  
- Receive actionable recommendations to improve fairness  

The platform provides a structured pipeline that combines statistical analysis, machine learning, and AI-powered explanations to ensure ethical and transparent decision-making.

---

## 5. Alignment with Challenge Objectives

Ethos AI directly aligns with the challenge objective:

> Build a clear, accessible solution to inspect datasets and software models for hidden unfairness and provide tools to measure, flag, and fix bias.

### How Ethos AI Meets This Objective

- **Inspection:** Evaluates datasets and model outputs using fairness metrics  
- **Measurement:** Quantifies bias through statistical indicators  
- **Flagging:** Highlights disparities across demographic groups  
- **Correction:** Suggests practical mitigation strategies  
- **Accessibility:** Presents results in an intuitive and understandable format

## 8. Technical Approach

Ethos AI follows a structured pipeline:

1. Data ingestion through user-uploaded datasets or model outputs  
2. Identification of sensitive attributes  
3. Computation of fairness metrics across groups  
4. Visualization of disparities  
5. AI-driven explanation of bias causes  
6. Recommendation of mitigation strategies  
7. Generation of a comprehensive fairness report  

This approach ensures a complete and repeatable workflow for bias auditing.
